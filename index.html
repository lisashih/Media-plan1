Our Strategic Framework: The Funnel

We will guide creators on a journey from discovery to submission. This full-funnel approach ensures we build a wide audience, then efficiently convert those most interested, maximizing our budget's effectiveness.

1. Awareness

Broadly reach potential creators to build campaign recognition and generate initial interest. The goal is mass reach within our target audience.

↓

2. Consideration

Drive qualified traffic to the campaign website. We'll engage users who have shown interest with more detailed content like categories, prizes, and judges.

↓

3. Conversion

Turn website visitors into actual submitters through strategic retargeting, clear calls-to-action, and urgency messaging near the deadline.

Campaign Timeline & Creative Phasing

The two-month campaign is divided into three distinct phases, each with a specific messaging focus and call-to-action to effectively guide the user journey.

Phase 1: Launch (Aug 1 - 21)

Focus: Awe & Opportunity

Build initial hype using stunning visuals from past winners. The goal is to establish credibility and capture broad interest.

CTA: "Learn More"

Phase 2: Mid-Campaign (Aug 22 - Sep 14)

Focus: Inspiration & Relevance

Showcase specific categories (Photo, Video, Digital Art) to spark ideas and make the competition feel more accessible. Drive qualified users to the site.

CTA: "Start Your Submission"

Phase 3: Final Weeks (Sep 15 - 30)

Focus: Urgency & Scarcity

Use clear countdowns and direct messaging. The goal is to convert high-intent users who have been procrastinating.

CTA: "Submit Now"

Plan Summary

This plan is designed to maximize submissions cost-effectively through data-driven decisions. The strategy is summarized by four core pillars:

Platform Strategy

We employ a "Core + Satellite" model. 50% of the budget is focused on Meta (IG/FB) as the core hub for the creative community, supplemented by Google for high-intent search, TikTok for youth outreach, and X (Twitter) for community amplification, ensuring total coverage and deep engagement.

Budget Strategy

The entire $60,000 budget is governed by achieving a Target CPA below $8.57. The budget is weighted towards the bottom of the funnel, with 40% dedicated to the most efficient 'Conversion' stage. The key to this plan is dynamic, weekly optimization based on data, not a rigid set-and-forget allocation.

Regional Strategy

We use a tiered investment to balance quality, volume, and cost. 60% of budget goes to higher-cost, mature Tier 2 markets (NA, EU) to ensure quality submissions, while 30% is allocated to lower-cost Tier 1 markets (IN, VN) to efficiently drive the total submission volume needed to hit our 7,000 target.

Timeline & Phasing

The two-month campaign (Aug-Sep) is phased into three stages: Launch (Awe & Opportunity), Mid-Campaign (Inspiration & Relevance), and Final Push (Urgency). Each phase uses distinct creative messaging and calls-to-action that align with the user's mindset, guiding them progressively toward submission.

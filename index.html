Interactive Paid Media Plan

The North Star: Our Core Objective

This entire plan is reverse-engineered from a single, primary objective: to generate the maximum number of quality submissions. Every decision is made to efficiently achieve our target Cost Per Acquisition.

Our Strategic Framework: The Funnel

We'll guide creators on a journey from discovery to submission using a classic full-funnel approach. This ensures we build a broad audience and then efficiently convert those with the highest interest, maximizing our budget's effectiveness.

1. Awareness

Broadly reach potential creators to build campaign recognition and generate initial excitement. The goal is mass reach within our target demographic.

↓

2. Consideration

Drive qualified traffic to the event website. We'll engage users who have shown interest with more detailed content about categories, prizes, and judges.

↓

3. Conversion

Convert site visitors into actual submissions through strategic retargeting, clear calls-to-action, and urgency-driven messaging as the deadline nears.

Campaign Timeline & Creative Phasing

The two-month campaign is structured in three distinct phases, each with a specific messaging focus and call-to-action to guide the user journey effectively.

Phase 1: Launch (Aug 1 - 21)

Focus: Awe & Opportunity

Build initial hype with stunning visuals from past winners. The goal is to establish credibility and capture broad interest.

CTA: "Learn More"

Phase 2: Mid-Campaign (Aug 22 - Sep 14)

Focus: Inspiration & Relevance

Showcase specific categories (Photography, Video, Digital Art) to spark creative ideas and make the contest feel more accessible. Drive qualified users to the site.

CTA: "Start Your Submission"

Phase 3: Final Weeks (Sep 15 - 30)

Focus: Urgency & Scarcity

Use clear countdowns and direct messaging. The goal is to convert high-intent users who have procrastinated.

CTA: "Submit Now"

KPIs & Risk Management

We will monitor a focused set of KPIs to measure success and have contingency plans ready to address potential challenges, ensuring our campaign remains agile and effective.

Key Performance Indicators

  • ✔

    Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)

    Our North Star metric. We'll optimize constantly to keep this below $8.57.

  • ✔

    Submission Volume

    The primary outcome. Monitored daily against our target pace.

  • ✔

    Website Conversion Rate

    Measures the effectiveness of our landing page at turning clicks into submissions.

Risks & Mitigation

Risk Mitigation Plan
High CPA Re-allocate budget from underperforming ad sets/regions to winners.
Low Conversion Rate A/B test landing page elements (headline, CTA) with the web team.
Ad Fatigue Deploy pre-made backup creative sets mid-campaign to keep content fresh.
